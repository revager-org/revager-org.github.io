RevAger Features

Organize review meetings in a simple way and keep track of them.
Manage the participants of a review very clearly.
Quick reviews: Immediately start recording the findings without any special preparation.
Supporting hints simplify the preparation and management of reviews in RevAger.
The participants can attend the review in different roles:
Reviewer, Scribe, Moderator, Author or Customer.
Create individual invitations for the participants of a certain review (PDF or ZIP).
Create, edit and sort findings and add references like images or screenshots.
Especially very convenient for reviews of user interfaces:
Add graphical annotations to the screenshots!
Compact view of the findings, so you don't lose track of them even if there are many findings.
Attach the corresponding aspects to the finding.
Manage the check lists and sort their aspects in categories.
Manage the aspects in one place and use them for arbitrary reviews.
Distribute the aspects to the reviewers automatically or manually.
Specify the product of the review and its version. Add references to the product.
The participants are stored in one place. You can easily add participants which already attended another review meeting.
Export the recorded findings as a nice-looking list of findings (PDF) or store them for further processing in XML or CSV.
Configure the export of CSV files by specifiying individual CSV profiles.
Specify all possible severites of a finding for each review individually.
The built-in stop watch helps to keep track of the duration of the current review meeting.
RevAger runs on all operating systems which support Java 6. The application is platform-independent.
At the moment RevAger is available in English and in German. We are using Launchpad Translations to translate the application. You can support us and help to make RevAger available in more languages very easily!
All data get stored in XML, so it's very simple to process them.
