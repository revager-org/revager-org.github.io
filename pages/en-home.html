Release of RevAger 1.4  ·  4 February 2011 | 6:05 pm

Release of RevAger 1.4

4 February 2011 | 6:05 pm

It's finally here: RevAger 1.4 with several improvements!

First of all we improved the overall usability by removing the modes paradigm. Now you have the freedom to work on a review like you need to do that. No change of modes anymore, just use all the functions directly!

In addition you can start a quick review even more quickly. Just select "Quick Review", specify the product and start creating new findings immediately!

The whole application is now completely available in English and in German. There are two sample checklists: "Software Specification" and "Second-hand Vehicle".

Download RevAger for Windows, Mac OS X, Linux and many other platforms:

https://launchpad.net/revager/+download
